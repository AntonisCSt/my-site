About me

Hello and welcome to my site!

I come from Greece and I am a Machine Learning Engineer with six years of experience in the data field. I am passionate about machine learning, data science, and AI-driven solutions.

I currently work at TUI Musement as a Machine Learning Engineer. In addition, I support a startup called Nanometrisis and take on freelance projects through Upwork.

I hold an Engineering Doctorate in Data Science from the Technical University of Eindhoven, Netherlands. During my time there, I collaborated with companies like Omron, ASML, TE Connectivity, and Brabant Water. I also have a Masters in Nanotechnology and a Bachelors in Applied Mathematics and Physics.

Lately, you can find me actively engaging with the DataTalksClub community on Slack. Beyond data and AI, I enjoy playing tennis and watching the NBA!
